What is Pravah

A fest built like a river

Pravah means flow — and that's exactly the shape of the fest. Three streams run in parallel across three days: a tech stream for builders, a culture stream for performers, and an open stream for whoever just wants to wander in.

No single stage, no single track. Pick a current and follow it, or drift between all three.

Stream 01

Code & Build

Hackathons, robotics, and a 24-hour build sprint.

Stream 02

Stage & Sound

Live bands, open mic nights, and the closing headline act.

Stream 03

Art & Ideas

Installations, talks, and a quiet corner for board games.
